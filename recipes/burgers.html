Odin Burgers

Description

Yummy Yummy Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1lb 85/15 Ground Beef
  • Burger Buns
  • Sliced American Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper

Steps

  1. Roll Patties into a Ball
  2. Flatten Patties on Grill
  3. Add Seasonings to Your Liking
  4. When Patties are Cooked, Add Cheese
  5. Prepare Buns
  6. Add Mayo, Mustard, Ketchup to Buns
  7. Add Patties to Buns
  8. Enjoy!
