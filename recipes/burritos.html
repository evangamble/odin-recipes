Odin Burritos

Description

Yummy Yummy Burritos

Ingredients

  • 1lb 85/15 Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream
  • Tabasco Sauce
  • Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Brown Ground Beef on Stovetop
  2. Add Seasonings to Your Liking
  3. Prepare Tortillas with Sour Cream and Guacamole
  4. Add Ground Beef to Tortillas
  5. Add Cheese to Tortillas
  6. Add Tabasco to Tortillas
  7. Enjoy!
