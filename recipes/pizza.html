Odin Pizza

Description

Yummy Yummy Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzerlla Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Pre-Heat Oven to 350°F
  2. Stretch Pizza Dough
  3. Add Sauce to Pizza Dough
  4. Add Cheese to Pizza Dough
  5. Place on Pan and Put in Oven for 10 min
  6. Enjoy!
